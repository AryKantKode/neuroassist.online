About Us - NeuroAssist

Revolutionizing Prosthetics with AI-Driven Technology

Welcome to NeuroAssist, where our mission is to transform the landscape of prosthetic technology and democratize access to high-quality healthcare. Our pioneering AI-driven EEG system is not just a product; it's a beacon of hope for millions.

Our Journey

Our story began at Chantilly High School, where founders Aryan and Gautham turned a casual conversation about AI into a passion project. With backgrounds in AI and neuroscience, and achievements like the Gold level status in the USA Computing Olympiad, our team is uniquely qualified to tackle this challenge.

Our Innovation

NeuroAssist stands out with its remarkable 99.9% accuracy in motor function classification. This leap in technology ensures our system adapts to diverse user needs, offering inclusivity in healthcare advancements. Our non-invasive, cost-effective solution makes advanced prosthetic technology accessible to all, regardless of socioeconomic background.
